21st Scout Jamboree

On December the 28th 6000 Scouts from all over New Zealand a few from Australia and some from the Cook Island meet up at Renwick in Malbrough from the Official 21st New Zealand Scouting Jamboree over the course of 10 days. The event involved Swiming, Rowing, Sailing, Kayaking, Abseiling, RockClimbing, Helicopters, Museem Visits,and a MASSIVE BATTLE.
